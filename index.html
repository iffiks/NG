ABOUT NAIMATH GARDEN

Naimath Garden is upcoming name in South India located in Parvathapur Village is a place in Zahirabad mandal, Hyderabad-Mumbai National Highway-9, Medak district of Telangana state, India. Mr. Mohammed Naimath, Founder and Sole-Owner, established Naimath Garden [NG] in the year 2015 with 25 acres of land. Property has traditional crops methods to produce, provide quality products to global consumers and contribute to healthy living.
