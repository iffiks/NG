HUT HOUSE


The Naimath Hut is an exotic alternative to the usual hotel accommodation. Our Thai Villas are built to exacting standards of traditional Thai style Houses, each equipped with a fabulous indoor garden bathroom. Enjoy the total privacy and comfort of individual quarters in a lush setting of fragrant flowering trees. The Naimath Hut provides the natural food fresh from garden and range natural services swimming pools and natural sauna.

Our Natural beverages are the specialties of the house, Naimath Hut has a jogging track of 0.5 km in the midst of nature, kids play area. There are wild rabbits plus many animals and birds that roam Naimath Hut,creating the “back to nature” atmosphere, the likes of which you’ll never find anywhere else.

15 minutes from Zaheerabad on the way to Hyderabad-Mumbai National Highway. From Hyderabad Airport 1 & 1/2 hour by taxi.
